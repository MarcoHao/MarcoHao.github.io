03. About

I’m a hybrid creator — part architect, part programmer, part AI tinkerer. My work lives at the intersection of physical form and computational logic.

Like the climbers I admire (such as Li Sanqiu), I believe design is a practice of persistent exploration. I don’t just build interfaces — I build systems that breathe, learn, and adapt.

When I’m not coding or modeling, you’ll find me wandering cities, photographing textures, or sketching in a notebook — always looking for the next connection.
